## Facilitation Insights

**What worked:**
- Name tag flip system for indicating desire to speak
- Bostrom/Andreessen pairing as readings
- Having an author of one reading as a participant (tangible discussion)

**Areas for improvement:**
- Enforce two-minute speaking rule more strictly
- Define terms and provide foundational context earlier
- Ensure optional readings are included in PDF packet
- Consider diversity of perspectives in participant selection
