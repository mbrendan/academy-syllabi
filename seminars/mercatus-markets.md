# Mercatus Mill Fellowship: On Markets

**Format:** Fellowship reading seminar
**Duration:** 6 sessions
**Location:** Washington, DC
**Partner:** Mercatus Center at George Mason University

---

## Overview

Part of the John Stuart Mill Fellowship program at the Mercatus Center, this seminar explores the philosophical foundations of markets, from Adam Smith's foundational insights through the socialist calculation debate to Hayek's theory of spontaneous order.

---

## Syllabus

**Readings:**

## About the Mill Fellowship

The John Stuart Mill Fellowship at the Mercatus Center brings together emerging scholars and practitioners to engage with classical liberal ideas and their application to contemporary policy challenges.

Fellows explore foundational texts in economics, political philosophy, and social theory through intensive seminars led by Mercatus scholars.
