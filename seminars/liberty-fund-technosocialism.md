# Liberty Fund Colloquium: AI and the Feasibility of Technosocialism

**Format:** Colloquium
**Duration:** 6 sessions
**Date:** February 13–16, 2025
**Location:** Savannah, GA (Andaz Savannah)
**Partner:** Liberty Fund

---

## Overview

This colloquium examines whether artificial intelligence could overcome the historic obstacles to socialist economic planning. From Hayek's knowledge problem to Lange's market socialism to Chile's Cybersyn project, we trace the debate over whether computation can substitute for markets—and what the implications are for human freedom.
