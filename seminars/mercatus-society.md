# Mercatus Mill Fellowship: On Society

**Format:** Fellowship reading seminar
**Duration:** 6 sessions
**Location:** Austin, TX (Omni Barton Creek)
**Date:** April 25–28, 2024
**Partner:** Mercatus Center at George Mason University

---

## Overview

Part of the John Stuart Mill Fellowship program at the Mercatus Center, this seminar explores the foundations of social order, moral sentiments, civil society, and the relationship between the individual and collective life.

---

## Syllabus

### Session 1: Sympathy and Moral Judgment

**Readings:**

**Smith, Adam. *The Theory of Moral Sentiments***
- Part I, Section I, Chapter 1, "Of Sympathy" (pages 9–13)
- Chapter 2, "Of the Pleasure of Mutual Sympathy" (pages 13–16)
- Chapter 3, "Of the Manner in Which We Judge of the Propriety or Impropriety of the Affections of Other Men, by Their Concord or Dissonance with Our Own" (pages 16–19)
- Chapter 4, "The Same Subject Continued" (pages 19–23)
- Section II, Chapter 3, "Of the Unsocial Passions" (pages 34–38)
- Chapter 4, "Of the Social Passions" (pages 38–40)
- Chapter 5, "Of the Selfish Passions" (pages 40–43)
- Section III, Chapter 2, "Of the Origin of Ambition, and of the Distinction of Ranks" (pages 50–61)
- Chapter 3, "Of the Corruption of Our Moral Sentiments, Which Is Occasioned by This Disposition to Admire the Rich and the Great, and to Despise or Neglect Persons of Poor and Mean Condition" (pages 61–66)
- Part II, Section I, Chapter 1, "That Whatever Appears to Be the Proper Object of Gratitude Appears to Deserve Reward; and That, in the Same Manner, Whatever Appears to Be the Proper Object of Resentment, Appears to Deserve Punishment" (pages 67–69)
- Section II, Chapter 2, "Of the Sense of Justice, of Remorse, and of the Consciousness of Merit" (pages 82–85)
- Chapter 3, "Of the Utility of this Constitution of Nature" (pages 85–91)

## About the Mill Fellowship

The John Stuart Mill Fellowship at the Mercatus Center brings together emerging scholars and practitioners to engage with classical liberal ideas and their application to contemporary challenges.
